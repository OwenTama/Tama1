❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my music to BeatZone for a review?

Fill in the contact form above, select "Album Review Request" as your subject and include a link to your music. Our editorial team reviews all submissions within 7 days.

Can I write a blog post for BeatZone?

Absolutely! We welcome guest contributors. Select "Blog Contribution" in the contact form and tell us your topic idea. We'll get back to you with our contributor guidelines.

How do I advertise on BeatZone?

For advertising and partnership enquiries, select "Advertising & Partnerships" in the form or email us directly at info@beatzone.co.ke for our media kit.

Is BeatZone available as a mobile app?

Not yet — but we are working on it! Follow us on our social media pages to be the first to know when the BeatZone app launches.
