📅 History of Music Genres

🎷
1920s

Jazz & Blues

The foundation of modern music. Jazz and Blues emerged from African American communities in the Southern United States, laying the groundwork for nearly every genre that followed.

🎸
1950s

Rock & Roll

Rock and Roll exploded onto the scene with Chuck Berry and Elvis Presley, electrifying a generation and changing youth culture forever.

🏝️
1960s

Reggae

Bob Marley and the Wailers brought Reggae from Jamaica to the world, spreading messages of love, unity and resistance through unforgettable rhythms.

🎤
1970s

Hip Hop

DJ Kool Herc threw a party in the Bronx, New York in 1973 and accidentally started a revolution. Hip Hop was born — a culture of DJing, MCing, breakdancing and graffiti.

🌍
2000s - Present

Afrobeats Goes Global

Artists like Wizkid, Burna Boy and Davido carried Afrobeats from Lagos nightclubs to the Grammy stage, making it the fastest growing genre in the world today.
